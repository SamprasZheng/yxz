# DotLeap / gbaci (Gilbert Bassey)

Author and publisher of **DotLeap** — the leading English-language Polkadot ecosystem newsletter. Published on Substack at [newsletter.dotleap.com](https://newsletter.dotleap.com). Handle: **gbaci**. Full name: **Gilbert Bassey**. The newsletter was originally co-founded with Bruno Škvorc (issues 41–46+ carried both bylines) before Bassey took it over as sole author.

## About DotLeap

Self-described as *"the most comprehensive weekly newsletter for the Polkadot ecosystem featuring updates on the relay chain, governance, parachains, tools, community, and more."* Mission: help readers *"stay in the loop about developments in Web3, Polkadot/Kusama, and Substrate without fragmentation over dozens of platforms."*

Format: weekly digest covering governance, parachain updates, DeFi, ecosystem project progress, funding decisions, and community developments. Also provides educational content on Substrate development, ink! smart contracts, and Polkadot dApp deployment.

## Editorial position

gbaci writes with an analytical, bullish-but-critical stance on Polkadot. Representative articles and themes:

- *"The Second Age of Polkadot"* — framing the ecosystem's 2025–2026 pivot as a generational reset
- *"Polkadot is inevitable"* — long-term conviction piece on the protocol's structural advantages
- *"Polkadot is winning the rollup game"* — argument that Polkadot's parachain model outperforms L2 rollup approaches
- *"Polkadot targets institutions"* — coverage of institutional DOT adoption dynamics
- *"100% EVM compatibility locked in"* — coverage of Polkadot Hub / PolkaVM (Revive) EVM parity
- *"Polkadot 2.0 complete"* — marking the completion of the 2.0 technical roadmap
- *"Polkadot GIGAHydration is live"* — DeFi/Hydration DEX milestone coverage
- *"Gavin on DOT-JAM relations"* — analysis of Gavin Wood's statements on JAM's relationship to DOT token value

Newsletter also covered:
- Alice und Bob's Q2 2025 Polkadot Treasury Report
- The March 14, 2026 DOT tokenomics shift (hard cap, DAP)
- PBA 2026 roadmap restructuring

## Positioning in ecosystem

DotLeap occupies the English-language information aggregator role — a curated weekly briefing for non-Chinese, non-developer audiences who want comprehensive ecosystem awareness without monitoring dozens of sources. It is a primary English-language reference alongside official Polkadot Forum posts.

## Authorship note

The original DotLeap was founded by Bruno Škvorc (Parity/RMRK ecosystem, now departed). gbaci co-authored early issues and has been the sole author since approximately 2022. The newsletter URL and Substack identity belong to gbaci/Bassey.
