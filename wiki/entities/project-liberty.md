# Project Liberty

Initiative advocating for user-owned, decentralized social networking infrastructure. Primary sponsor of [[concepts/dsnp]] (Decentralized Social Networking Protocol) and the [[entities/frequency]] blockchain that hosts DSNP data.

## Key facts

- **Mission:** Break the data-monopoly model of incumbent social platforms; give users portable identity and graph ownership
- **Flagship protocol:** [[concepts/dsnp]] — promoted as a cross-platform standard for social graphs
- **Targeted platforms:** Pushes DSNP interoperability across TikTok, Bluesky, and [[entities/frequency]]
- **Governance angle:** Collaborates with [[entities/audrey-tang]] on [[concepts/plurality]]-aligned governance design

## Why it appears here

The author of [[sources/plurality-audrey-tang-sampras-2025]] first encountered *Plurality* via Project Liberty's documentation on cross-platform DSNP expansion, which led to discovering Audrey Tang's involvement.
