# Huatong PCB (華通電腦)

## Basic Information

- Taiwan PCB manufacturer
- Global market leader in LEO satellite-dedicated PCBs

## LEO Satellite Positioning

**~80% global market share in LEO satellite-dedicated PCBs** (as of end of 2025).

| Financial Metric | Value |
|---------|------|
| 2025 Q4 LEO satellite board revenue | **>NT$15.5 billion (all-time high)** |
| Global market share | ~80% |

## Competitive Advantages

Satellite PCBs have far higher requirements for reliability, radiation resistance, and thermal cycling than consumer electronics; certification barriers form a strong moat. As global LEO investment grew from $25 billion in 2024 to $45 billion in 2025, Huatong is a direct beneficiary.
