## Polkadot coverage

Coverage is primarily from **2021** (bull market cycle) and focuses on price analysis and entry-level explainers:

- **DOT as ETH innovation thesis**: Positioned DOT as comparable to ETH in the next cycle; distinguished it favorably from EOS as "innovation" vs. "imitation."
- **Infrastructure metaphor**: Described Polkadot as a "high-end shopping mall" providing infrastructure — tenants (dApps) benefit from its shared security and interoperability.
- **Bear market floor debates**: Community-run polls on DOT price floors; acknowledged sharp post-ATH drawdowns likely after bull run.
- **Technical tutorial content**: Bilibili video on Polkadot multisig functionality (since deleted).
- **TA (2021)**: Technical analysis calling DOT potentially bullish but warned of stacked consolidation patterns.
