# PolkaWorld

Largest Polkadot community in Asia, operating since June 2019. Founded by **Xiaojie** (Xiao Jie Pang, founder of HashBang, a blockchain knowledge community) and **Guanghua Guo** (co-founder of ChainX, the first Substrate-based chain). Self-described as "The Largest Polkadot Community in Asia Since 2019" and the "Only Recipient of Web3 Foundation Community Grant." X: [@polkaworld_org](https://x.com/polkaworld_org). Medium: [polkaworld.medium.com](https://polkaworld.medium.com).

## Background & Founding

- Established June 2019, co-sponsored by HashBang and ChainX
- Originally built community around Polkadot and Substrate development education for Chinese audiences
- Published an early online course "Blockchain from 0 to 1" with over 200,000 copies sold
- Received 12 consecutive Polkadot Treasury funding approvals — the most consistent community-funding track record in the ecosystem
- Also operates an English X account alongside Chinese content

## Governance history

PolkaWorld experienced a significant funding crisis (approximately 2022–2023) when an OpenGov referendum for ~16,842 DOT (~$80,000 for 3 months) received only 6.7% approval despite the organization having received ~$694,000 in prior Treasury funding. The failure reflected broader ecosystem tension: ordinary DOT holders feared that approving large community grants would reduce token stability, creating headwinds for all ecosystem proposals. PolkaWorld overcame this crisis and continued operating.

## 2025–2026 Operations (Referendum 1522)

Submitted operational proposal for April 2025 – March 2026 requesting $266,652 USDT ($22,221/month). The referendum passed with **80.4% support** (~58.42M DOT aye).

Committed deliverables:
- 288 articles on Polkadot/ecosystem topics
- 72 livestreams (Chinese + English audiences)
- 48 educational short videos
- 12 in-depth interviews
- Minimum 3 posts/day on each of their two X accounts (Chinese and English)

Delivered in prior year (exceeded targets):
- 324 articles published (vs. 288 committed)
- 71 livestreams with **39,027 unique viewers**
- English X: 1.9M impressions, 5.5% engagement rate
- Chinese X: 819,500 impressions, 6.5% engagement rate
- Co-hosted Gavin Wood's four-city **JAM Tour in mainland China** (with Pala Labs) — 874 confirmed attendees

## Role in governance

PolkaWorld participated as a **first-cohort Decentralized Voices (DV)** representative, voting on 54 proposals. They co-hosted Gavin Wood's four-city JAM Tour in mainland China.

Xiaojie Pang also sits on the Harbour Industrial Capital funding advisory board alongside other Polkadot ecosystem members.

## Editorial position

PolkaWorld translates key Polkadot content into Chinese and produces original analysis. Representative X posts:
- Commentary on the March 14, 2026 DOT hard cap / tokenomics shift: *"DOT moves toward a 2.1B hard cap, inflation is reduced, and DAP reshapes treasury and incentive flows"*
- Coverage of PBA's 2026 roadmap restructuring: *"more focused on @paritytech talent + a product-driven direction"*
- Year-end reflection translation: *"Polkadot is actively preparing for non-crypto-native [users]"*
