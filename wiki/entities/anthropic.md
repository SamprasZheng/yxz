# Anthropic

US AI safety company; creator of the Claude model family. Founded 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and other ex-OpenAI researchers.

## Key Products (2026)

- **Claude family** — Haiku (fast/cheap), Sonnet (balanced), Opus (flagship), Fable (reasoning), Mythos (extended reasoning); model-tier naming adopted early 2025.
- **Claude Code** — Agentic CLI and IDE extension for software engineering tasks.
- **Claude Science** — Research-optimized vertical for pharmaceutical R&D (launched 2026-06-30).
- **Claude Cowork** — Collaborative workplace product.

## Milestones

- 2026-03: Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 hit US export control restrictions (jailbreak-linked, June 12 to June 30).
- 2026-06-30: US lifts export controls; Fable 5 globally restored July 1 with "extraordinarily strong safeguards."
- 2026-06-30: Claude Sonnet 5 launched — most agentic Sonnet; becomes default in Claude Code July 1.
- 2026-07: California state agencies deal — all agencies/cities/counties Claude access at 50% discount.
- 2026-07: Anthropic removing hidden Claude Code detection codes added to catch unauthorized resellers and prevent model distillation.
