# Anthropic

US AI safety company; creator of the Claude model family (Haiku / Sonnet / Opus / Fable); founded 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and colleagues from OpenAI. Operates on a Public Benefit Corporation structure with a Long-Term Benefit Trust as oversight body.

Key milestones tracked in this wiki:
- Claude Fable 5 returned to global availability July 1, 2026 under a joint Amazon/Microsoft/Google safety-classifier framework.
- AMD investment up to $5B announced July 20, 2026.
- IPO investor meetings underway as of July 2026.
- Claude Code: enterprise AI coding CLI with FedRAMP High government edition, MCP live-data connectors, and in-app browser (July 2026).
- Products: Claude API, Claude for Education, Claude for Government, Claude Science, Claude Code.
