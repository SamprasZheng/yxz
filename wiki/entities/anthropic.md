# Anthropic

Anthropic PBC — AI safety company and creator of the Claude model family. Founded 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and other ex-OpenAI researchers. Headquartered in San Francisco.

## Key products

- **Claude** — flagship LLM family; current lineup (June 2026): Claude Fable 5 + Mythos 5 (Mythos-class, 1M-token context), Claude Sonnet 4.6, Claude Opus 4.8, Claude Haiku 4.5
- **Claude Code** — CLI and IDE agent for software engineering; nested sub-agents, advisor tool (June 2026)
- **Managed Agents** — scheduled agentic deployments via the developer platform
- **Claude Partner Network** — SI/consulting partner program; Services Track launched June 3, 2026

## Recent milestones (2026)

- Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 released June 2026; 1M-token context, always-on adaptive thinking
- Claude Sonnet 4 + Opus 4 retired June 15, 2026
- Advisor tool (fast executor + high-intelligence advisor) in public beta for long-horizon agentic workloads
- DXC Technology integration into banking/airline systems announced June 11, 2026
