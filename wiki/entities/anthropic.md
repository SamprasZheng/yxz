# Anthropic

US AI safety company founded 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and colleagues who departed OpenAI. Develops the Claude model family (Haiku / Sonnet / Opus / Fable / Mythos); operates Claude.ai, Claude Code, and Claude Cowork. Known for Constitutional AI (CAI), the Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP), and safety-first model development. On track for $47B annualized revenue in 2026, overtaking OpenAI.

Key 2026 milestones: Claude Fable 5 + Mythos 5 subject to US export controls June 12, restored globally July 1 after controls lifted; Claude Sonnet 5 (1M-context) launched June 30 as default for all Free/Pro users; California Governor Newsom partnership for state agencies; jointly proposing industry jailbreak-severity scoring framework with Amazon, Microsoft, Google.
