# Anthropic

US AI safety company founded 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and former OpenAI team members. Maker of the Claude model family. Headquartered in San Francisco.

## Key facts

- Founded 2021; revenue on pace for $10.9B in Q2 2026 (>2× prior quarter); approaching first profitable quarter (Bloomberg, May 2026)
- 80× QoQ revenue growth reported Q1→Q2 2026; leasing inference capacity at xAI's Colossus cluster (Memphis) to handle demand (Fortune, May 2026)
- Models: Claude 3 family (Opus/Sonnet/Haiku) → Claude 4 family (Opus/Sonnet/Haiku); primary frontier-model competitor to GPT series
- Products: Claude.ai, Claude API, Claude Code (CLI + web IDE + IDE extensions), Claude Managed Agents (public-beta self-hosted sandboxes + MCP tunnels research preview, May 2026)
- Strategic partnerships: KPMG (276K employees, 2026), Amazon AWS, Google GCP; Microsoft not a primary cloud partner
- Acquisitions: Stainless (API SDK tooling, 2026)
- Project Glasswing: Claude Security public beta for vulnerability scanning and triage (security teams)
