# Anthropic

US AI safety company founded 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and colleagues who departed OpenAI; maker of the Claude model family. Focused on AI safety research alongside capability development.

## Claude Model Family (2026)

- **Claude Fable 5** (June 2026) — safer general-use flagship; stronger coding, vision, memory, long-context
- **Claude Mythos 5** (June 2026) — trusted-access tier with expanded safeguards; suspended by US government order 2026-06-12
- Prior Claude 4.x family (Opus 4.8, Sonnet 4.6, Haiku 4.5)

## Key Developments (2026)

- Confidential draft S-1 filed with the SEC 2026-06-01 (IPO process underway)
- US government directive suspended Mythos 5 access 2026-06-12 — most significant AI safety regulatory enforcement action to date
- Publicly urged AI labs to pause and warned of human loss of control (2026-06-05)
- TCS and DXC partnerships expanding Claude into regulated industries (banking, airlines)
