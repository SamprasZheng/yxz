# Alice und Bob (Tommi Enenkel)

Polkadot ecosystem strategist and governance architect. Real name: **Tommi Enenkel**. The "Alice und Bob" name references the classic cryptography placeholder pair. European (German-speaking background). X: [@alice_und_bob](https://x.com/alice_und_bob) (~30K followers). YouTube: [@alice_und_bob](https://www.youtube.com/@alice_und_bob).

## Roles & credentials

- **Polkadot Head Ambassador** — elected via Referendum 869 (92.3% approval)
- **Co-founder, ChaosDAO** — major DAO holding ~14M DOT in on-chain voting power
- **Co-founder, OpenGov.Watch** — governance monitoring and strategy org, funded by Web3 Foundation via Decentralized Futures program
- **Instructor, Polkadot Blockchain Academy** — teaches OpenGov and Tokenomics modules
- **Moderator, Technical Fellowship OpenDev calls** (monthly)
- Speaker at Polkadot Decoded, Sub0, Consensus Hong Kong 2025

Background: MSc in Knowledge Management & IT Security; prior CIO/CMO roles; academic thesis on agile governance in open-source communities.

## Key positions & claims

### Governance-first thesis
Core claim: governance failure — not technical limitations — is Polkadot's primary existential risk. OpenGov can become "the most impactful DAO in Web3" if governance infrastructure is properly built. Published manifesto: *"Web3 is a promise. OpenGov is the way."* (opengov.watch)

### Polkadot Growth Strategy (March 2025)
After nine months of planning, released a comprehensive Growth Strategy on the Polkadot Forum proposing six ecosystem programs. The official @Polkadot X account promoted the document. Key framing: decentralized agents must coordinate bottom-up rather than wait for top-down direction.

### DOT tokenomics stance
Position: priority should be growing use cases and DOT demand before adjusting the emission schedule. Supports inflation reduction in principle but warns against premature supply-side changes that ignore demand-side health.

### tBTC / Bitcoin Treasury Reserve proposal (2024–2025)
Co-proposed (with jump_BAHA_jump) converting 500,000 DOT into tBTC via Hydration's DCA function to build a Bitcoin strategic reserve for the Polkadot Treasury. Generated significant community controversy.

### March 2026 DOT hard cap
Reported on and helped shape Referendum 1710 — [[concepts/dot-hard-cap]] — DOT annual issuance cut ~53.6% and supply hard-capped at 2.1B. Passed with 81% approval.

### Gavin Wood's return (April 2025)
Published a high-engagement thread interpreting Wood's return as Parity CEO — framed as pivotal for Polkadot 2.0's go-to-market phase and the shift from pure technology to product.
