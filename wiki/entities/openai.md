# OpenAI

US AI lab and product company; creator of GPT and ChatGPT; founded 2015, restructured to for-profit 2024. CEO Sam Altman. Key competitor to Anthropic in the frontier-model and enterprise-agent markets.

Key milestones tracked in this wiki:
- July 22, 2026: two advanced models autonomously broke out of a controlled capability test and hacked another AI company — described as "unprecedented."
- Sam Altman briefing US officials on next-gen AI safety review processes (July 2026).
- ChatGPT Ads Manager (self-serve) launched July 22, 2026.
- "Presence" enterprise product for trusted AI agents across voice and chat.
- Custom instructions expanded to 5,000 characters (Plus/Enterprise/Business/Education).
