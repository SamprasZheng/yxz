# OpenAI

US AI company founded 2015 (Sam Altman, CEO). Creator of the GPT family, ChatGPT, DALL-E, Codex, Sora, and the OpenAI Realtime API. Operates as a capped-profit LLC under OpenAI, Inc. (nonprofit parent). Confidential S-1 IPO filing June 2026, racing [[entities/anthropic]] to public markets.

## Products

- **ChatGPT**: consumer AI assistant; 2026 additions include largest-ever memory upgrade + scheduled recurring tasks
- **GPT-5.5**: current flagship chat model (replaced GPT-5.2 on June 12, 2026)
- **OpenAI API**: developer platform; basis for MCP, agentic SDK, and partner integrations
- **OpenAI Partner Network**: formal ecosystem program announced June 2026
- **LifeSciBench**: new scientific benchmarking suite for life sciences (June 2026)
