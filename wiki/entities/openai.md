# OpenAI

San Francisco AI research company; creator of the GPT series and ChatGPT. Co-founded by Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, and others (2015); Elon Musk departed early; restructuring to for-profit corporation ongoing as of 2026.

## Key facts

- **Products:** ChatGPT; GPT API; DALL-E; Whisper; Codex / GitHub Copilot (via Microsoft partnership)
- **Models (as of June 2026):** GPT-5.5 Pro + Instant (current default); o-series reasoning models; GPT-4.5 retiring June 27, 2026
- **Governance:** Frontier Governance Framework published May 29, 2026; trustworthy third-party evaluation playbook
- **Deployment Company:** OpenAI Deployment Company launched to help businesses build around intelligence
- **Personal agents:** Peter Steinberger (OpenClaw founder) joined OpenAI Feb 2026 to lead personal-agents division

## Competitive context

Anthropic filed for IPO ahead of OpenAI in June 2026, taking market-value and revenue lead. Both companies racing toward public markets as the AI-infrastructure cycle matures.
