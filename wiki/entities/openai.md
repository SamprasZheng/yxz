# OpenAI

US AI research company (founded 2015 as non-profit, now "capped-profit"); creator of GPT and o-series model families, ChatGPT (≥600M weekly users as of 2025), and the OpenAI API. Primary sponsor of the [[concepts/openclaw]] Foundation (via [[entities/peter-steinberger]] hire, Feb 2026). Projects $25–33B ARR in 2026. Launched GPT-5.6 (Sol/Terra/Luna family) in limited government preview July 2026; Sol at $5/$30 per Mtok, running at up to 750 tok/sec on Cerebras.

Key competitor to [[entities/nvidia]] (infrastructure), Anthropic (frontier closed models), and Google DeepMind. Revenue-projection lead lost to Anthropic in mid-2026 ($47B vs $25–33B ARR).
