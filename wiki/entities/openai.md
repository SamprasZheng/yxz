# OpenAI

OpenAI — AI research company and creator of the GPT/ChatGPT/Codex/DALL-E/Sora product family. Founded 2015 by Sam Altman, Elon Musk (departed 2018), Greg Brockman, and others. Headquartered in San Francisco; for-profit capped PBC restructure completed 2025.

## Key products

- **ChatGPT** — consumer/enterprise AI assistant; current model: GPT-5.5 (as of June 12, 2026; GPT-5.2 retired)
- **GPT-OSS** — open-weight models (120B + 20B, Apache 2.0); released August 2025
- **Codex** — code-execution agent; used for scientific simulation use cases
- **Sora** — video generation
- **Operator / Agents SDK** — agentic product tier

## Recent milestones (2026)

- Confidential S-1 draft filed with SEC June 10, 2026 (pre-IPO milestone)
- Ona acquisition announced June 12, 2026 (scope undisclosed)
- GPT-5.2 deprecated → GPT-5.5 auto-migration (June 12, 2026)
- ChatGPT cross-session memory system upgraded; rolling out to Plus/Pro US
- OpenAI Economic Research Exchange launched June 8, 2026
- Oracle Cloud: OpenAI models available via Oracle cloud commitments
