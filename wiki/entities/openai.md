# OpenAI

AI research and deployment company (San Francisco); creator of the GPT model family, ChatGPT, Codex, and the OpenAI API. Transitioned from non-profit to capped-profit structure; Microsoft is a major investor and distribution partner via Azure OpenAI Service.

**Key products:** GPT-5 / GPT-5.5 (language), o-series reasoning models, ChatGPT (consumer + enterprise), OpenAI API, Codex (agentic coding), Sora (video), Whisper (ASR), DALL·E (image).

**June 2026 milestones:** Frontier models + Codex launched on AWS Bedrock GA for commercial and GovCloud regions (June 2); GPT-5.5 / GPT-5.5 Pro / GPT-5.5 Instant family tracked; GPT-4.5 retiring from ChatGPT June 27; o3 retiring from ChatGPT August 26 (API unaffected); OpenAI Deployment Company launched to help enterprises build around intelligence.
