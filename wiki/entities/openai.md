# OpenAI

US AI company founded 2015 (non-profit → capped-profit → for-profit conversion 2025); develops the GPT model family (GPT-4 → GPT-5 → GPT-5.6 Sol/Terra/Luna) and the ChatGPT consumer product. Primary financial sponsor of the [[concepts/openclaw|OpenClaw Foundation]] (established Feb 2026). [[entities/peter-steinberger]] (OpenClaw creator) joined as personal-agents lead Feb 14, 2026. Annualized revenue ~$25–33B in 2026.

Key 2026 milestones: GPT-5.6 family (Sol flagship, Terra capable, Luna fastest) launched behind government-managed access list — first frontier model gated by government coordination; preliminary discussions about giving the US government a 5% equity stake across leading AI developers; competitive pressure from Anthropic ($47B) and Chinese open-weight models (Z.ai GLM-5.2).
