# OpenAI

AI research company founded 2015; creator of the GPT model family and ChatGPT. Structured as a capped-profit company (converted 2025). Major products: ChatGPT (consumer), Codex (coding agent), GPT API.

## Key facts

- **Models**: GPT-5, GPT-5.5 Instant (released May 2026), o-series reasoning models, Codex
- **Products**: ChatGPT (free / Plus / Pro / Team / Enterprise), Codex (coding agent), Sora (video), Whisper (ASR), DALL-E
- **API**: OpenAI-compatible REST API; widely re-implemented (NemoClaw port 8642, OpenClaw, LM Studio, etc.)
- **Codex**: Recognised in 2026 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Enterprise AI Coding Agents

## Recent (2026)

- **GPT-5.5 Instant** — shipped May 2026; emphasis on clarity and personalisation
- **ChatGPT personal finance** — Pro users can connect financial accounts; spending dashboard + grounded Q&A
- **ChatGPT Healthcare** — AdventHealth integration for workflow automation and admin burden reduction
- **Klarna shopping engine** — inside ChatGPT, pulling 100 M+ products across 13 markets in real time
- **Netflix ad-buy agents** — Netflix hands autonomous ad-spot buying to ChatGPT-powered agents
