# OpenAI

OpenAI is an AI research company and the creator of the GPT model family and ChatGPT, headquartered in San Francisco.

## Key facts

- **Founded**: 2015; restructured to a for-profit PBC in 2024
- **Products**: ChatGPT, GPT-5.x model family, Codex (coding agent CLI + IDE), DALL-E, Sora, Operator (web-browsing agent), OpenAI API
- **Models (June 2026)**: GPT-5.5 + GPT-5.5-Cyber (released June 2026); GPT-5.4 mini (rolling out Free/Go); GPT-4.5 retiring June 27 from ChatGPT
- **IPO**: Confidential S-1 filed with SEC on May 22, 2026; targeting September 2026 public listing
- **Key personnel**: Sam Altman (CEO); Peter Steinberger joined Feb 2026 as personal-agents division lead (see [[entities/peter-steinberger]] / [[concepts/openclaw]])

## Relation to this wiki

OpenAI's GPT models are the primary alternative to Nemotron in the Spacesharks/Firefly agent stack; the Codex agent CLI is a direct competitor to Claude Code. OpenAI's personal-agents division (led by Steinberger) is the institutional home of the OpenClaw community post-Feb 2026.
