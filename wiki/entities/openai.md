# OpenAI

AI research and deployment company founded 2015; creator of the GPT and ChatGPT product lines. Restructuring from non-profit to for-profit capped-benefit corporation. As of June 2026: last private valuation $852 B (March 2026 round, $122 B raised); confidential S-1 draft filed with the SEC June 8, 2026 (Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, JPMorgan as lead underwriters); targeting fall 2026 IPO at >$1 T valuation; racing [[entities/anthropic]] to public markets.

## Key Products (2026)

- **ChatGPT** — consumer chat; improved persistent cross-session memory (June 2026)
- **GPT-5.5** — latest closed frontier model; SWE-Bench Pro 58.6%
- **gpt-oss-120b / gpt-oss-20b** — open-weight models (Apache 2.0, Aug 2025)
- **Codex** — agentic software-engineering product
- **ACP (Agent Communication Protocol)** — co-developed with Stripe; Apache 2.0 (Sep 2025); one of the five agentic-payment protocols in [[concepts/agentic-payment-protocols]]
- **OpenAI Economic Research Exchange** — launched June 8, 2026; studies AI's macroeconomic impact
