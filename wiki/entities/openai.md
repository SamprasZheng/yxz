# OpenAI

US AI company founded 2015; creator of GPT model family, ChatGPT, DALL-E, Sora. Transitioned from non-profit to capped-profit/PBC structure 2025. Products: ChatGPT (consumer + enterprise), API (GPT-4/5 family), Codex, OpenAI Agents SDK.

**2026 notable developments:**
- GPT-5.6 family released (July 2026); ChatGPT Work agent for multi-step research + document creation.
- Atlas discontinued; all products consolidated under ChatGPT as a single super-app.
- Apple v. OpenAI trade-secret lawsuit filed July 10, 2026 — targeting ex-Apple hardware staff Tang Tan and Chang Liu plus Jony Ive's IO Products ($6.5B acquisition 2025).
- Confidential IPO filing with Goldman Sachs + Morgan Stanley (target debut Sept 2026, ~$730B valuation).
- Price war with Meta on token costs for GPT-5.6 and open-weight models.
