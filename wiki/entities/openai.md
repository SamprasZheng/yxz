# OpenAI

US AI company; maker of ChatGPT / GPT model family; founded 2015 as non-profit, converted to capped-profit; $852B valuation (July 2026); annualized revenue $25–33B; expects profitability 2030.

Key products: ChatGPT (Free/Plus/Team/Enterprise), GPT API, GPT-5.6 family (Sol flagship / Terra / Luna, launched July 2026), OpenAI o-series reasoning models, Codex, DALL-E.

Notable 2026 events: GPT-5.6 Sol/Terra/Luna launch (stronger reasoning, coding, biology, cybersecurity); preliminary talks to grant US government 5% equity stake; GeneBench-Pro computational biology benchmark; Peter Steinberger joined as personal-agents lead (Feb 2026); OpenAI's $25-33B annualized revenue trails Anthropic's ~$47B pace.
