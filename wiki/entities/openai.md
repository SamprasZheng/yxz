# OpenAI

US AI company; creator of GPT and ChatGPT. Founded 2015, converted to capped-profit structure 2025; CEO Sam Altman.

## Key Products (2026)

- **GPT-5.6 family** (July 2026, limited preview) — Sol (flagship on Cerebras, up to 750 tok/s), Terra (lower-cost), Luna (fastest); follows GPT-4o/o3 lineage.
- **ChatGPT** — Consumer + Business + Enterprise; major distribution channel for GPT models.
- **OpenAI API / Responses API** — Developer platform; MCP server integration added 2025.
- **Operator** — Consumer agentic product; browser + computer use.
- **OpenClaw Foundation** — Primary financial sponsor of the [[concepts/openclaw]] open-source agent runtime (Feb 2026–); [[entities/peter-steinberger]] joined as personal-agents lead Feb 14, 2026.

## Milestones

- 2026-02-14: Peter Steinberger joins OpenAI; OpenClaw Foundation established with OpenAI as primary sponsor.
- 2026-07-02: OpenAI proposes 5% US government stake as part of broader arrangement covering leading US AI developers.
- 2026-07: GPT-5.6 (Sol/Terra/Luna) in limited partner preview; public launch expected mid-to-late July.
- 2026-07: GeneBench-Pro benchmark released for AI agents in computational biology.

## Six-Region Position

US closed-frontier leader; primary commercial rival to [[entities/anthropic]]. Open-weight play: GPT-oss and Llama-4 contributions (both open-as-funnel, closed-as-moat strategy per [[synthesis/open-weight-llm-agent-stack-six-region]]).
