# OpenAI

US AI company founded 2015; maker of ChatGPT and the GPT model series. Headquartered in San Francisco. Originally a non-profit; converted to capped-profit structure 2019; IPO filing imminent (May 2026).

## Key facts

- Founded 2015 by Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, Elon Musk (departed 2018), and others
- CEO Sam Altman; CTO Mira Murati departed 2024; Peter Steinberger (OpenClaw founder) joined Feb 2026 to lead personal-agents division (see [[entities/peter-steinberger]])
- Models: GPT-4 → GPT-4o → GPT-4.5 → GPT-5.5 (launched May 2026; most capable to date per OpenAI)
- Products: ChatGPT (consumer + Pro + Enterprise), OpenAI API, Operator (computer-use agent), o-series reasoning models
- IPO: Confidential S-1 filing with Goldman Sachs + Morgan Stanley expected late May 2026; September 2026 public debut targeted (Bloomberg, TechCrunch, CNBC, May 2026)
- Cloud: Microsoft Azure primary cloud historically; exclusivity partially relaxed in 2026 — now distributing via Amazon Web Services; Google partnership in discussion
- ACP (Agent Commerce Protocol): OpenAI + Stripe initiative for agentic payments (see [[concepts/agentic-payment-protocols]])
