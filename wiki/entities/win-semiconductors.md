# Win Semiconductors (穩懋半導體, Stock: 3105)

## Basic Information

- Taiwan-listed company (3105)
- World-leading compound semiconductor wafer foundry, specializing in GaAs / GaN PA (power amplifiers)

## LEO Satellite Positioning

**Global leader in LEO-spec PA foundry services.** Major LEO constellations such as Starlink and Amazon Kuiper are among Win Semiconductors' key customers for RF front-end PA chips.

With Starlink V3 upgrades (more laser inter-satellite links, higher power requirements) and future RF demands from AI computing satellites, Win Semiconductors is one of the most direct upstream beneficiaries in the LEO supply chain.
