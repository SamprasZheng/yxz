# Bill Laboon

Vice President of Ecosystem at Web3 Foundation (promoted November 2025). Previously Director of Education and Governance Projects. X: [@BillLaboon](https://x.com/BillLaboon). LinkedIn: [linkedin.com/in/billlaboon](https://www.linkedin.com/in/billlaboon/).

## Background

- Former five-year lecturer in Computer Science at the **University of Pittsburgh**
- Software career spanning: consultant, QA engineer, field engineer, technical lead, manager
- Author of:
  - *"A Friendly Introduction to Software Testing"* — undergraduate textbook used at 10+ colleges/universities
  - *"Strength in Numbers"* — science fiction novel exploring cryptocurrency's societal impact
- Interest in blockchain emerged ~2010 through analysis of hashing algorithms for decentralized storage; became active ~2012
- Joined Web3 Foundation as Head of Education and Grants; later Director of Education and Governance Projects

## Role at Web3 Foundation

### Previous role (until November 2025): Director of Education & Governance Projects
Responsible for:
- OpenGov-related work and governance participation
- Technical education content (Polkadot Wiki, Web3MOOC — a 20-week blockchain fundamentals course)
- Coordinating support and staking teams
- Decentralized Voices program coordination
- Kusama Council membership

### Current role: VP of Ecosystem (from November 2025)
Announcement (X, 2025):
> *"I am happy and proud to announce that I have accepted a new role as Vice President of Ecosystem at W3F. I am looking forward more than ever to helping grow the Polkadot ecosystem!"*

The promotion brought ecosystem development, funding mechanisms, community collaboration, and technology promotion under a unified strategic mandate. Introduced at the time of the **Polkadot Agents Program** launch.

## Polkadot Agents Program

Launched alongside Laboon's VP appointment. A micro-grant fast-track system: certified "Polkadot Agents" can apply directly to W3F for small grants (~$500) processed quickly without full Treasury referendum overhead. Framed as "cement binding the ecosystem together."

## Key positions

- **Decentralization is a spectrum**: *"True Decentralization functions as an ideal rather than an achievable absolute — decentralization is a spectrum with inherent trade-offs."*
- W3F mission: *"Web3 Foundation helps build the decentralized web — a future where people own and control their own data."*
- Active participant in Polkadot OpenGov since 2025 — W3F began voting more visibly in referenda
- Speaker at Merge Buenos Aires (2025): *"Scaling Web3 with 44 Blockchains — Polkadot's Vision"*
- Regular podcast presence: Crypto Altruists (episodes 205, 238), Blockchain Bylines, The Dots podcast

## Relevance

Laboon is the most institutionally prominent Polkadot educator and ecosystem coordinator — the face of Web3 Foundation's public-facing education and governance initiatives. His VP promotion signals W3F shifting to a more active, coordinating role in the ecosystem post-Gavin Wood's return.
