# Polkadot Roundup 2025

Annual retrospective written by Gavin Wood on the Polkadot Medium, covering 2025 milestones and the 2026 outlook.

**Source:** https://medium.com/polkadot-network/polkadot-roundup-2025-3c3c71c7e9c4

## 2025 Technical Milestones

### The Great Hub Migration
- Transferred **1.6 billion DOT** across **1.53 million accounts** in approximately 8 hours
- Result: 100× lower fees, 2-second block times
- Represents the move to "Polkadot Hub" as the central smart-contract-capable system chain

### Polkadot Hub (Revive / PolkaVM)
- Native smart contracts via the **Revive** project
- Dual compatibility: full EVM bytecode support *and* native PolkaVM for higher performance
- Enables both Solidity developers and new-paradigm Polkadot-native developers

### Elastic Scaling
- Parachains can dynamically utilize more coretime for burst processing demand
- No longer constrained to a single core's throughput ceiling

### Enterprise-grade metrics
- P95 transaction latency: **28 ms**
- In-block confidence: **99.8%**

### Multi-Chain Rally (early 2026)
- Network hit **100,000+ TPS without raising user fees**

## 2026 Roadmap Items (from Roundup)

| Feature | Target |
|---|---|
| NOMT technology | ~10× TPS improvement |
| 500ms BASTI blocks | 2026 |
| CoreChain Phase 1 | 2026 |
| JAM mainnet | Post-2026 (testnet and stabilization phase in 2026) |

## Strategic shift

Wood frames 2026 as the year Polkadot must move from being a *technology platform* to a *product platform* — demonstrable, end-user-facing Web3 applications for non-crypto audiences.

Sub0 Buenos Aires (2026) talk title: *"The Second Coming of Polkadot."*
