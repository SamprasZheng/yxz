# 波卡解碼EP3: Hydration去中心自造市 DAMM

**Author:** [[entities/polkasharks]] | **Date:** 2024-09-02 | **Series:** 波卡解碼

## Summary

Deep-dive into Hydration, a DeFi protocol on Polkadot featuring an Omnipool AMM. Author frames Hydration's liquidity rewards as a "survive the bear market" strategy.

## Key claims

- Hydration uses an Omnipool (single pool for all assets) — higher capital efficiency than traditional multi-pool AMMs
- Gas fees described as "extremely low"
- Liquidity reward APYs range from 4% to 75%+; rewards denominated in vDOT and HDX
- Polkadot ecosystem injected 1,000,000 DOT into the Omnipool as bootstrap liquidity
- LPs received 200%+ APY during the initial incentive period; second round expected to last one year
- Author treats yield as a survival strategy during market downturns
