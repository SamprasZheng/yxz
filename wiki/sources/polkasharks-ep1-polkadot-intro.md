# 波卡解碼EP1: Polkadot - 下一代的區塊鏈

**Author:** [[entities/polkasharks]] | **Date:** 2024-08-26 | **Series:** 波卡解碼

## Summary

Introductory article in the PolkaSharks "波卡解碼" (Polkadot Decoded) series. Explains Polkadot's architecture, compares it to Ethereum, and previews three upcoming developments: a Polkadot ETF, Polkadot Pay, and JAM.

## Key claims

- Polkadot uses a relay chain + parachains architecture; the relay chain provides shared security and consensus, parachains are independently optimized blockchains
- Native token: DOT; governance via OpenGov allowing DOT holders to initiate and vote on referenda
- Created by Gavin Wood (Ethereum co-founder, Yellow Paper author)
- Polkadot described as "friendlier" than Ethereum — faster transactions, significantly lower gas fees
- 21Shares applied for the first global Polkadot-focused ETF with the SEC (as of 2024)
- Polkadot Pay (Sept 2024): developed by Parity, Novasama, Raise — staking + up to 20% cashback rewards
- [[concepts/jam]] gray paper published Q2 2024; expected Q2 2025 launch; aims to replace parachain protocol

## Practical demo

Author walked through a live cross-chain flow: Moonbeam rewards → StellaSwap swap → XCM transfer to Hydration → HDX cold storage. Demonstrates [[concepts/xcm]] protocol in action.
