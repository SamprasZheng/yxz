# Roadmap

This roadmap is explicitly post-MVP. It should not leak back into the hackathon scope.

## Purpose

The MVP proves a narrow loop: public space-weather or conjunction signals can be turned into traceable recommendations at low cost. The roadmap expands that loop across additional operator concerns.

## Phases

- Phase 1: ground segment and link-quality awareness
- Phase 2: thermal and mechanical advisory
- Phase 3: telemetry and anomaly small-model layer
- Phase 4: lessons-learned corpus
- Phase 5: constellation and fleet view

## Use rule

- Do not extend Phase 0 with roadmap items
- Log future ideas here instead of expanding the hackathon scope
