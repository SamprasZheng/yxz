# Polkadot Decoded EP5: Decentralized Physical Infrastructure — DePIN and peaq

**Author:** [[entities/polkasharks]] | **Date:** 2024-09-09 | **Series:** Polkadot Decoded

## Summary

Introduces peaq — a Layer 1 blockchain on Polkadot optimized specifically for DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks) and RWA (Real World Assets). Explains how physical IoT devices can participate in decentralized economies.

## Key claims

- peaq is a Layer 1 built on Substrate/Polkadot, optimized for DePIN and IoT
- Leverages parallel block generation, async backing, and agile coretime
- Integrates "the largest Ethereum and Substrate developer ecosystems in Web3"
- Native token: **$PEAQ** (already issued as of article date)
- Use case: physical devices (EVs, charging stations) participate in decentralized networks via blockchain
- Economic model: inflation + transaction fee distribution incentivizes network participation
- Author characterizes it as enabling a "true IoT economy"
