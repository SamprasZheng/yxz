# Fleet Strategy

Spacesharks is Starlink-first. The demo should focus on a representative Starlink sample, find the few objects that need attention, and produce a short evidence-backed fleet brief.

## Initial sample

- Start with 50 Starlink satellites
- Keep the pipeline expandable to 100
- Select from public Starlink TLE data
- Refresh at a practical cadence
- Treat MEO and GEO satellites as reference objects, not the core product

## Demo target

- Most objects remain green
- A small number become yellow
- One or two objects become red
- Red objects include evidence, confidence, disagreement, and a recommendation
- Disputed red cases route to `Nemotron`
