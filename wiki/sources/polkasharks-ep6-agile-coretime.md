# Polkadot Decoded EP6: The Foundation of Polkadot 2.0 — Agile Coretime

**Author:** [[entities/polkasharks]] | **Date:** 2024-09-23 | **Series:** Polkadot Decoded

## Summary

Explains Agile Coretime — Polkadot 2.0's replacement for parachain slot auctions. Three modes of coretime access; lower barrier to entry for developers.

## Key claims

- Agile Coretime replaces the old parachain slot auction model
- Three modes:
  1. **On-demand coretime** — immediate resource access, billed per use, ideal for short-term/testing
  2. **Bulk coretime** — 28-day advance purchase of stable resources, suitable for large projects
  3. **Secondary market** — unused coretime can be bought/sold, optimizing cost management
- Lowers barriers for new developers and small projects
- Provides cost predictability and faster deployment
- Author frames it as the "foundation of Polkadot 2.0"
- Elastic Scaling is the next step beyond Agile Coretime — further efficiency gains
