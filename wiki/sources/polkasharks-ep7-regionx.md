# 波卡解碼EP7: RegionX - 區塊鏈算力交易

**Author:** [[entities/polkasharks]] | **Date:** 2024-10-07 | **Series:** 波卡解碼

## Summary

Introduces RegionX Coretime Hub — the secondary market for Polkadot and Kusama coretime. Explains primary and secondary market mechanics for buying/selling computational block space.

## Key claims

- RegionX is the coretime secondary market for Polkadot and Kusama
- Primary market offers two types: on-demand (immediate, per-block) and bulk (advance scheduled)
- Secondary market has two mechanisms:
  1. **Direct access** — priced by region lifecycle; active regions use dynamic pricing reflecting depreciation
  2. **Orders** — crowdloan-style, users contribute DOT (not stake); parachains can offer rewards
- Allows purchasing less than a full core — significantly lowers operational costs vs. full slot auctions
- Framework: "Polkadot is launching a new 'coretime' system for deploying parachains on its network"
