## 5 · Figure of Merit (FOM)

### Formula

```
FOM = 0.4 × confidence
    + 0.3 × normalized_sizing_sigma
    + 0.2 × recent_hit_rate
    + 0.1 × news_momentum
```

Each component normalized to **[0, 1]** before weighting:

| Component | Weight | Source | Normalization |
|-----------|--------|--------|--------------|
| `confidence` | 0.40 | LLM thesis output (0–1 float) | Already [0,1] |
| `sizing_sigma` | 0.30 | Scan output; raw σ from volatility model | Min-max across watchlist each day |
| `recent_hit_rate` | 0.20 | Rolling 5-day hit rate from backtest log | Count(correct) / Count(total) |
| `news_momentum` | 0.10 | News scout sentiment score (−1 to +1) | `(score + 1) / 2` |

**Design rationale:** Confidence dominates (model quality signal), sizing_sigma captures asymmetric opportunity, hit_rate provides Bayesian correction from realized outcomes, news_momentum is lowest weight as it's the noisiest/most gameable signal.

**Future iterations to consider:**
- Replace min-max on sizing_sigma with CDF rank (more robust to outliers)
- Add a staleness penalty if news_momentum data is >48h old
- Introduce a regime factor (VIX bucket) to scale confidence weight down in high-volatility regimes
