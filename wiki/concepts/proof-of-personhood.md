# Proof of Personhood (PoP)

A proposed replacement for Polkadot's current staking mechanism, announced by [[entities/gavin-wood]] at the 2025 Berlin Web3 Summit. Uses on-chain decentralized human validation to distinguish real individuals from bots and AI agents.

## Problem it solves

Current staking concentrates governance power with large DOT holders. PoP shifts influence from token-weighted voting to human-weighted validation — one verified human = meaningful participation rights, regardless of stake size. Also addresses AI/bot manipulation of governance at scale.

## Three-stage rollout

| Stage | Name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| DIM1 | Proof of Individuality | Basic uniqueness: prove you are not the same person as any other registered participant |
| DIM2 | Proof of Verified Individuality | Layer on real-world identity signals (details TBD) |
| DIM3 | TBD | Further verification or privilege tiers |

## Governance connection

Gavin Wood filed his first personal treasury proposal to fund PoP's initial deployment — framed as restarting Polkadot's "human-centric growth" trajectory. The mechanism is intended to complement [[concepts/dot-hard-cap]] (supply scarcity) with genuine demand from verified human users.

## Status

- Announced July 2025 (Berlin Web3 Summit)
- As of early 2026: DIM1 rollout in progress; DIM2/3 timeline TBD
