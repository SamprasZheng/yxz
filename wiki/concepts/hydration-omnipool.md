# Hydration Omnipool

Hydration's next-generation AMM (Automated Market Maker) on Polkadot. A single unified liquidity pool for all assets — higher capital efficiency than traditional multi-pool designs.

## How it works

Traditional AMMs (like Uniswap) use separate pools per trading pair (e.g., DOT/USDC, ETH/DOT). Hydration's Omnipool **merges all assets into one pool**, which:
- Reduces slippage by concentrating liquidity
- Allows any-to-any swaps in a single transaction
- Lowers gas costs (Polkadot-native, fees described as "extremely low")

## Token

**HDX** — Hydration's native governance and liquidity incentive token.

## Liquidity incentives

Per [[sources/polkasharks-ep3-hydration]]:
- LP APYs: 4% to 75%+ (denominated in vDOT and HDX)
- Polkadot ecosystem injected 1,000,000 DOT as bootstrap liquidity
- Initial incentive LPs received 200%+ APY; second round ~1 year duration
