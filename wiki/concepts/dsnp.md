# DSNP — Decentralized Social Networking Protocol

Open protocol for portable social graphs and user-owned social identity, championed by [[entities/project-liberty]] and hosted on the [[entities/frequency]] blockchain.

## Core idea

Move the social graph (identity, follows, content references) out of platform silos and into a shared, decentralized substrate so users — not platforms — own and port their network.

## Ecosystem positioning

- **Sponsor:** [[entities/project-liberty]]
- **Reference chain:** [[entities/frequency]] (Polkadot-adjacent blockchain optimized for DSNP throughput)
- **Target integrations cited:** TikTok, Bluesky, Frequency — discussed in [[sources/plurality-audrey-tang-sampras-2025]]
- **Governance philosophy:** Aligns with [[concepts/plurality]] — multi-stakeholder, inclusive decision-making rather than single-platform control

## Why it matters

DSNP is the social-protocol counterpart to chain-level governance experiments like [[entities/polkadot|Polkadot OpenGov]]: both attempt to replace centralized gatekeeping with transparent, user-participatory systems.
