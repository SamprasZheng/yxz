# DGX Spark

DGX Spark is the small-form-factor NVIDIA reasoning workstation mentioned in the [[sources/nvidia-agent-challenge-2026]] stack context. In this wiki it belongs to the local/edge deployment layer for long-running agents: powerful enough for agent demos and reasoning workloads, but still close to the developer workstation flow rather than a cloud-only cluster.
