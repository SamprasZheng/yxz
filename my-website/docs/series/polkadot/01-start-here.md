## Why a Series?

The blog is sorted in reverse chronological order, which is unfriendly to readers who want to go deep on a single topic — new and old articles are mixed together, and the same topic may be scattered across three years. The `Series` section is the thematic reading version:

- **Polkadot learning path** (this page)
- RF / hardware engineering path (TBD)
- Space × AI investment path (TBD)

The latter two are still being organized and will be added later.
